Scholarship Distribution

Our Story

The scholarship program began in 1996 supporting 300 students initially. Over the years, the Trust has steadily expanded its reach across Rajasthan, supporting thousands of girls pursuing education and empowerment through financial aid.

We remain committed to breaking financial barriers so deserving students can access quality education without hurdles.

Schools and Colleges Covered

The scholarship program supports students across 35 schools and 17 colleges located in Jodhpur, Barmer, and other districts to ensure wide educational outreach.

  • Shri Vivekanand Senior Secondary School, Jodhpur
  • Government Girls School, Barmer
  • Swami Vivekanand College, Jodhpur
  • Shastri College, Barmer
  • Government Polytechnic, Jodhpur
  • Central Higher Secondary School, Jodhpur
  • Rajasthan Women’s College, Barmer
  • Navodaya Vidyalaya, Jodhpur
  • Government Arts College, Jodhpur
  • Local vocational training centers
