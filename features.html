Unique Features Of The Trust

Education Without Discrimination.

No girl wishing to study should be deprived of education for want of money irrespective of her marks, religion, caste, etc.

Empowering Girls Through Education—From Schools to Colleges.

We are the ONLY TRUST to provide financial support for education to needy girls to a wide range of schools (35) and colleges (17) of Jodhpur and Barmer districts of Rajasthan.

Education Without Boundaries

The financial support is exclusively need-based. Financial Support provided to the needy girls irrespective of their academic record, religion, caste, etc.

Transparent Student Selection Criteria

The selection of the needy students is made by the class teacher through their application forms recommended by the Principal to the trust. The payment of School fees, Board fees and Scholarships are also made through cheques in the name of school. The whole amount is distributed amongst the students in the presence of higher authorities of the education department.

Every Penny Directly Supports Girls

Administrative expenses are kept as low as possible. They are voluntarily borne by the trustees.

Beyond Education: Mind, Body, Soul

Not only trust imparts financial support for education but also teach morality and ‘sanskar’ through personality development sessions that includes physical, mental, emotional and spiritual development.

Transforming Potential into Results

These academically poor students when joined the Trust and Sanskar classes exhibited marvelous work and excelled in their academic records due to sincere efforts and guidance of the Trust members.

Rising with Knowledge and Confidence

There was marked improvement in the academic achievements of these girls. These girls never looked back and now they are moving ahead with confidence and ability to face future challenges in life.

Guiding Girls Towards Holistic Growth

Trust provides guidance and support for the overall growth of the girls. Therefore, they are taught about their duties along with their rights so that they can be responsible citizens and contribute to the development of the country.
These students equipped with education and Sanskar are now ready to face future challenges.

Empowering Girls For Lifetime

Most of the Trusts provide financial support only. The unique feature of the trust is a strong professional backup support. This trust provides financial support and opportunity for overall growth. Trust develops a sense of belongingness among all the girls. They consider themselves as a member of this trust family, where highly qualified educated and renowned members of the trust, in different walks of life, provide strong and powerful professional back up support not only during education period but interviews, career counselling and during their entire life.
