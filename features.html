Unique Features of the Trust

No girl is denied education due to lack of money—regardless of marks, religion, or background.

The Trust supports girls across 45 schools and 37 colleges in Jodhpur and Balotra districts.

Financial help is extended solely on the basis of need, not on academic performance.

Students are selected through a structured process involving teachers and principals, with fee payments made directly to institutions.

Administrative costs are minimal and borne voluntarily, ensuring donations directly benefit students.

The Trust not only provides financial support for education but also instills moral values and Sanskar through personality development sessions that nurture physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual well-being.

Many underperforming students have achieved academic excellence through the Trust's consistent guidance.

Students supported by the Trust are now confident, capable, and ready to take on life’s challenges.

The Trust offers holistic guidance and support to foster the all-round development of its students. Alongside their rights, girls are also taught their duties—empowering them to become responsible citizens who actively contribute to the nation’s progress. Equipped with education and strong moral values (Sanskar), these young women are now prepared to face future challenges with confidence and purpose

Most of the Trusts provide financial support only. The unique feature of the trust is a strong professional backup support. This trust provides financial support and opportunity for overall growth. Trust develops a sense of belongingness among all the girls. They consider themselves as a member of this trust family, where highly qualified educated and renowned members of the trust, in different walks of life, provide strong and powerful professional back-up support not only during education period but interviews, career counselling and during their entire life.
