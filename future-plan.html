Future Plans

Due to paucity of funds, financial assistance for higher and technical education is partial. It is our endeavor that the trust should be financially so strong/capable that it can bear the entire financial expenditure of each needy girl's education, especially higher education

To provide assistance and financial support to these girls to take professional degree courses in national level institutions outside Jodhpur.

To prepare shortlisted girl students for administrative services through professional coaching classes.

Promote Women Entrepreneurship

To get these girl students educated in national and international level educational institutions

To get the Trust recognized at the national level, i.e. to establish branches of the Trust in different parts of the country apart from Jodhpur and Balotra

Due to shortage of funds, it is currently not possible to implement these plans in totality/completely. However, these goals would definitely can be achieved in future with the support and guidance of like-minded people.

The culmination/ultimate goal of all this work in the direction of women empowerment is to develop these students into good human beings who can leave a distinct imprint on society and become a source of inspiration for others. This will prove to be a concrete step towards building a great society and nation.
