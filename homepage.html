Swami Vivekanand Students’ Welfare Charitable Trust

Empowering every girl with education and values, beyond financial barriers.

Our Mission

To ensure that no girl is deprived of education due to financial constraints, irrespective of marks, religion, or caste. Beyond financial aid, we nurture values, character, and personality development so that these students grow into responsible citizens and contribute meaningfully to society
