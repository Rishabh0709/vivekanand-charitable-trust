Swami Vivekanand Students’ Welfare Charitable Trust

Dedicated to promoting education and empowering girl students in Rajasthan since inception.
Our mission is to build a brighter future through relentless support, scholarships, and community-driven programs.

Our Mission

To uplift and empower girl students through accessible education and dedicated support across Rajasthan,
fostering a culture of excellence, inclusivity, and social transformation.
