Basic Infrastructure Support

Bhaiya Shri Nandkishore Sharda realized that unless basic facilities are provided along with financial assistance, this effort for education will remain incomplete. Therefore, in order to resolve the basic problems of the schools, Shri Nandkishore Sharda got furniture, classrooms, reception rooms, toilets, water-huts, boundary walls built in the schools and relieved the girls from an atmosphere full of neglect, disregard and scarcity

Shri Sharda himself provided financial support for the construction of furniture, classrooms, reception halls, water-huts, toilets and boundary walls in many schools and also inspired other trust members to follow suit. He also took help and support from the District Collector, Members of Parliament (MP), Members of Legislative Assembly (MLA), etc.

Constructed the Shri Nandkishore Sharda Reception Hall and three classrooms in Mahalaxmi Girls' School, Pratapnagar, Jodhpur

Shri Nandkishore Sharda, Kumari Basanti Manihar and other associates of the Trust provided financial support for the construction of "Kumari Basanti Manihar Commerce Block" in Mahila Mahavidyalaya, Jodhpur

Constructed “Tulsi Water-hut” in 2 Girls School in Jodhpur in fond memory of his mother, Smt. Tulsi Devi

The trust arranged the furniture for 16 classrooms in 7 girl’s schools

Apart from these, Trust also constructed Halls, Classrooms, Computer Labs. etc. worth over 1.5 crore rupees in various schools including Siwanchi Gate, Alpbhasha Rajmahal, Jalori Gate, BhagatkiKothi, Rajmahal, Mahamandir and Sardarpura through the support of MPs, MLAs, etc.
