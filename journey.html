27 Years of Journey: Important Milestones

1998

Trust Founded

Swami Vivekanand Students’ Welfare Charitable Trust was established by Shri Ram Prasad Sharma with the mission of empowering girls in Rajasthan through education.

2002

First Scholarship Awarded

The inaugural batch of scholarships was granted to 15 deserving students, setting the foundation for future educational support.

2007

Community Workshops Launched

Initiated skill development and awareness workshops across multiple rural and urban communities to empower beneficiaries.

2012

Partnership with Local NGOs

Collaborated with various non-governmental organizations to expand outreach and resource availability.

2018

10,000th Beneficiary Milestone

Celebrated the support of 10,000 girls through scholarships, mentorship programs, and community engagements.

2023

Expanded Urban Outreach

Initiated extended programs in urban centers, focusing on vocational training and digital literacy for girls.

2025

Future Growth & Adaptation

Launching new women empowerment initiatives while continuing to evolve scholarship programs to meet modern challenges.
