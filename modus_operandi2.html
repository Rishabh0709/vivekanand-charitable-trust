Modus Operandi
Shri Nandkishore Sharda's intense thinking and contemplation led to a methodology for uplifting needy girl students, addressing not just financial support but also their values, confidence, and holistic well-being.
Financial Assistance

To provide financial assistance to these needy girl students, ensuring that lack of resources does not hinder their education.

Inspire Human Values & Confidence

Address the girls to instill human values, boost their confidence, and strengthen their foundation by distributing inspirational literature.

Awaken Curiosity Towards Education

Bhaiya ji strongly believed that curiosity towards education should be awakened. He explained the true importance of learning beyond simply clearing exams or passing grades.

Instill Compassion & Spirituality

Instill basic values in the girl students such as compassion, tolerance, positivity, gratitude to God and Nature, and encourage selfless prayers daily for developing a well-rounded character.

Promote Health, Hygiene & Well-being

Emphasize how vital health is, especially for these girls from areas with poor standards of sanitation and hygiene. Bhaiya Shri Nandkishore Sharda advocated simple ways to be healthy — fresh air, water, sunlight, smiling, pranayama, yoga, exercise, morning walks, and nutritious food—alongside benefits for future generations.

Develop Intellect & Discretion

With foresight, Bhaiya Shri Nandkishore Sharda understood that awakening and developing intellect-discretion (buddhi-vivek) is essential for girls to claim their place in society and mold their futures.

Foster Duties, Rights & Good Citizenship

Encourage girls to embody both rights and duties—becoming aware, responsible citizens who carve out their own future and contribute positively to society.

Synergy of Stakeholders

Recognize that lasting success comes from the combined effort of parents, teachers, and the girl students themselves. Bhaiya ji ensured the collaboration of all stakeholders in empowering these girls.
