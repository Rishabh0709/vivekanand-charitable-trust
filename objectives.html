Our Objective

Creative thinking & inquisitiveness

Develop original and creative thinking, and inquisitiveness in the students so that they can face the challenges of the future

Educational Assistance

Arouse the interest of students towards education, to guide them how to study efficiently, how to score good marks and how to solve the problems faced in studies, and to provide them with text books and other study material.

Self-Esteem, Self-Confidence, Positive Thinking

Instill the feeling of self-esteem, self-confidence and self-dependency, and to develop positive and science-based thinking, and restraint of speech in the students.

Living happily and peacefully

Teach students the art of living happily and peacefully while maintaining balance in life even in adverse circumstances

Freedom from mental stress

Impart guidance/knowledge for achieving freedom from mental stress arising out of the present glittering materialistic era.

Holistic Growth

Provide knowledge and guidance to students for personality development which is necessary for their holistic development

Respect for nature & environment awareness

Develop respect for Mother nature and create awareness about environment and energy conservation.

Self-Employment & Entrepreneurship

Motivate the students for self-employment and entrepreneurship development.

Rights and Duties

Make the students aware of their rights as well as their duties so as to make them ideal citizens.
