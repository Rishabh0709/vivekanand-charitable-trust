Scholarship Distribution

Our Story

The scholarship program began in 1996 supporting 300 students initially. Over the years, the Trust has steadily expanded its reach across Rajasthan, supporting thousands of girls pursuing education and empowerment through financial aid.

We remain committed to breaking financial barriers so deserving students can access quality education without hurdles.

Scholarship Selection Criteria

Eligible students must be female and currently enrolled in any recognized school, college, or university.

Financial need should be substantiated by income certificates or relevant official documents.

Student Selection Procedure

1
Application Submission

Students submit scholarship applications with complete documents by the deadline.

2
Document Verification

The Trust verifies financial and academic documents submitted by applicants.

3
Shortlisting Candidates

Eligible candidates are shortlisted based on criteria and document checks.

4
Final Selection & Awarding

Selected students receive scholarship awards and follow-up support.
