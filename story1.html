Neetu Deora
Senior Assistant, SBI, Shastri Nagar Branch , Jodhpur (Raj.)

I received support from the Swami Vivekanand Students Welfare Charitable Trust for my education from 9th class (1998) through to the completion of my M.Com. in 2007. I am deeply grateful to the Trust for standing by me during one of the most challenging periods of my life—after the loss of my father.

At that time, continuing my education seemed impossible. Our family was going through an emotionally and financially difficult phase, and had it not been for the timely support from the Trust, I would not have been able to pursue my studies. The education I received during those crucial years not only shaped my career but also helped me earn a dignified place in society.

Since 2008, I have been working at State Bank of India (SBI), and I am currently serving as a Customer Assistant. The values and guidance imparted to me—especially by Maa Basanti Manihar, the Founder and President of the Trust—have had a lasting impact on my life. Her wisdom and support have also helped me build and maintain a happy and peaceful married life.

As a humble gesture of gratitude, I regularly contribute financially to the Trust. It is my way of giving back to the institution that helped shape my future and continues to uplift many others.

I extend my heartfelt thanks and warm regards to Maa Basanti Manihar and Maa Madhubala Advani for their tireless dedication and for making such a positive difference in the lives of countless girls. Their unwavering support and guidance have been instrumental in shaping many futures, including mine.
