Success Story
Neetu Deora
Manager, SBI, Shastri Nagar Branch , Jodhpur (Raj.)
I studied through the help of this trust from 9th class (1998) to M.Com. (2007). I am much thankful to the trust because it helped me during the adverse phase of my life when my father expired. During that difficult time, if the trust had not helped me, I would not have been able to pursue my studies. It is only because of my education through the trust that I have got a dignified place in society.

I am working in SBI since 2008. Currently, I am working as Customer Assistant. Due to guidance from Maa Basanti Manihar, the founder and President of the Vivekanand Trust, I am able to lead a happy married life. I give my warm regards to Maa Basanti Manihar for making my life so peaceful. I very humbly regularly contribute financially to the trust as a token of my gratitude towards the trust.
