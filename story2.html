Poonam Mishra
Principal,Wisdom International School, Jodhpur

I became a part of the Swami Vivekanand Students Welfare Charitable Trust in 1998, while studying in Class 6. From that point until the completion of my M.Sc. in 2010, the Trust generously supported all my educational expenses.

As one of four siblings in a financially struggling household, our situation became especially challenging when my father lost his job due to the closure of the Alcobex Factory in Jodhpur. Managing even basic household needs and rent became a daily struggle, and continuing our education seemed out of reach.

At that crucial juncture, the Trust became a beacon of hope. Their timely intervention not only lifted the financial burden but also instilled in me the confidence and determination to pursue my academic goals with renewed dedication.

Today, I am honored to serve as the Principal of Wisdom International School in Bhagat Ki Kothi, Jodhpur. This milestone in my life would not have been possible without the unwavering support and visionary guidance of Maa Basanti Manihar, the Founder and President of the Trust.

I am equally thankful to Shri Nandkishore Ji Sharda and Maa Madhubala Advani for their encouragement and support throughout my journey. Their belief in me helped transform my aspirations into reality.

I will always remain deeply grateful to the Trust for shaping not just my career, but my character and values. It is because of their selfless service that I now have the opportunity to give back and contribute to society.
