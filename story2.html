Success Story
Poonam Mishra
Principal,Wisdom International School, Jodhpur
I joined Swami Vivekanand Students Welfare Charitable Trust in 1998 when I was in 6th class.
All expenses of my studies from 6th class to M.Sc. (2010) were borne by this trust.

The financial condition of our house was not good, we were four siblings. Father used to work in Alcobex factory(Jodhpur) and then due to closure of the factory, it was difficult to pay the household expenses and house rent. At that time, our studies would have been discontinued if there was no trust. Today, it is because of this trust that I was able to get higher education.

Hence, I am very grateful to Maa Basanti Manihar who is the founder and president of this trust and also our guide. Today I am working as the Principal in Wisdom International School, Bhagat Ki Kothi, Jodhpur. Thanks to Maa Basanti Manihar and Bhaiya Shri Nandkishore Ji Sharda who fulfilled my dreams.
