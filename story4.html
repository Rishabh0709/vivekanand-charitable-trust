Success Story
Dr. Kumudini Dhadhich
Lecturer, Rajasthan Education Dept.
Currently, I serve as a Chemistry Lecturer in the State Education Department (since 2016).

I have been a recipient of the trust’s financial support from Class 6 to my PhD (2012). During Class 10, I was on the verge of leaving my studies due to my family’s financial hardships. At that critical time, the trust not only provided financial assistance but also instilled values, inspiration, and spiritual strength, which helped me persevere.

Today, I hold a respectable position in the education department, and I am committed to passing on the knowledge, education, and values I received from the trust to future generations

I am deeply grateful to Swami Vivekanand Students Welfare Charitable Trust for shaping my career and life.
