I am currently serving as a Chemistry Lecturer in the State Education Department since 2016. My educational journey has been deeply supported by the Swami Vivekanand Students Welfare Charitable Trust, which provided financial assistance from Class 6 to my PhD (2012).

During my Class 10, my family faced severe financial difficulties, and I was on the verge of discontinuing my studies. It was during this critical phase that the Trust came to my aid, not only with financial help but also with much-needed inspiration, spiritual strength, and values that empowered me to keep going.

Thanks to the Trust, I was able to complete my education and today hold a respectable position in the education department. I remain committed to passing on the knowledge, education, and life lessons I received from the Trust to future generations of students.

I am deeply grateful to the Swami Vivekanand Students Welfare Charitable Trust for shaping both my career and my life, and I hope to continue giving back in the same way I was supported.
