Scholarship Selection Criteria

How Trust Selects Students

1. School Girls ▶

The selection of needy students is made by the class teacher through application forms, recommended by the Principal to the trust.

  • Payments of school fees, board fees and scholarships are made via cheque in the name of the school.
  • Amounts are distributed amongst students in presence of higher education authorities.
2. College / Competitive Exams ▶

Once a girl completes school education, trust volunteers reach out individually (via postcard/call) to:

  • Understand their future plans
  • Provide career guidance
  • Offer scholarship (part or full)

How Students Can Seek Support

1. School Girls ▶

Reach out to class teacher or principal and explain financial condition. Once principal approves and provides a scholarship form, the trust will grant financial aid.

2. College / Competitive Exams ▶

If a girl wishes to pursue higher studies or competitive exams but cannot afford expenses, she can directly visit the trust’s office. Volunteers will:

  • Assess financial condition
  • Collect student details
  • Ensure support from trust or community-based programs
