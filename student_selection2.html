How Trust Selects Student

  • School Girls:
    The selection of needy students is made by class teachers from application forms recommended by the Principal to the trust.
    School fees, board fees, and scholarships are paid via cheques directly to the school, and funds are distributed among students in the presence of higher education officials.
  • College / Competitive Exams:
    After school, trust volunteers connect individually (postcard/call) to understand the student’s future plans, provide career guidance, and offer full or partial scholarship support as needed.

How Students Can Find This Trust

  • School Girls:
    Students should approach their class teacher or principal to discuss financial needs. If approved, they submit the scholarship form to the trust for scholarship assistance.
  • College / Competitive Exams:
    Girls planning higher education or competitive exams and needing help can visit the trust’s office directly. Volunteers assess their financial situation and provide support, either from the trust or community scholarship programs.
