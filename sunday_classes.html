Personality Development Classes

Why These Classes Were Started

Shri Nandkishore Sharda enkindled the flame of education in girls suffering from financial constraints and leading a purposeless/aimless life. It is true that the education system of India is inadequate, monotonous and aimless in itself, but necessary for livelihood. Shri Shardaji took the responsibility for holistic development of such girls and now Maa Madhubala Advani and other members of ‘Manidweep’ are taking it ahead

To accomplish this, Personality Development Classes are organized every Sunday from 10:30 to 12:30 from July to December every year for students from all walks of life. Here, they are taught the importance and purpose of education and human life, importance of the Almighty God, the art of striking balance in family and society, etc. and also enlightened on many small and big things useful in life.

Why Personality Development is Important

Personality development is a vital component of a student’s growth. It boosts confidence, enhances communication skills, fosters leadership qualities, and improves emotional intelligence. These classes help students handle challenges with resilience and positivity, preparing them to excel in both academic and social settings.

Students learn vital life skills such as time management, stress management, and positive thinking, all important to becoming balanced, successful individuals.

How These Classes Help in Holistic Development

The classes help students grow not only academically but also mentally, socially, and emotionally. Through interactive activities, group discussions, and skill-building exercises, students become more self-aware, socially adept, and confident individuals.

The training encourages students to embrace challenges, manage emotions constructively, and develop a positive outlook on life — essential qualities for success in today’s dynamic world.
